What is Evolution?

Evolution is change over time. As organisms adapt, they develop and become different fundamentally. There are many different factors inflicting the change. One of them is the environment. The environment affects how the organism lives. The organism will adapt to find food, water, and shelter. Many of the basics decided how the organisms were changed and how they adapted. Another of the determining factors was natural selection. As humans, we chose the food that looked the best and planted more of that same plant. Eventually all the plants began to change.
