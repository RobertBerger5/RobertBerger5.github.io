Human Evolution

Evolution Evaluated

What is Evolution?

Evolution is change over time. As organisms adapt, they develop and become different fundamentally. There are many different factors inflicting the change. One of them is the environment. The environment affects how the organism lives. The organism will adapt to find food, water, and shelter. Many of the basics decided how the organisms were changed and how they adapted. Another of the determining factors was natural selection. As humans, we chose the food that looked the best and planted more of that same plant. Eventually all the plants began to change. All the food became bigger. That’s how humans had cultivated a food surplus, allowing us to get to where we are today. Many different things have to do with evolution. Everything alive has evolved from a common ancestor.

Human Lives

Humans, what are we? Where did we come from? We, as humans, are united as one. We come from a chain of events unraveling on after another It’s hard to believe that we used to be different, that we all came from a common ancestor. So many things had evolved from one. Our relatives and ancestors are not human beings. We evolved differently and separately from them. To be human is to be evolved, cultured, and intelligent. We are always learning new information. We will never stop. Life will never stop.
